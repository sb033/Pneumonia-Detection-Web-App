This is a deeplearning web app which helps to predict if a patient is having TB/pneumonia or not with the help of CNN(convolutional neural network).

This app is designed to analyze a chest x-ray and predict the results.

In the results NEGATIVE is for Normal and POSITIVE is for Pneumonia

The dataset to train our model was taken from kaggle.The dataset has train and test dataset consisting of Normal and Pneumonia X-RAYS images which used for training CNN.

For our research purpose we have also referred the article by Antani SK at National Library of Medicine(NLM)

  • Chest X-Ray Images (Pneumonia)
  • Tuberculosis Chest X-ray NLM
